ABOUT DISHLY

Ever travel to somewhere new and want to try the local cuisine? Or maybe you're just curious about what dishes are popular in your area. Unfortunately, Yelp won't help you very much. Since it's a business-oriented application, there is no way to determine which dishes are the most popular or most highly rated in your city. Instead, try Dishly! Dishly is a web application based off of Yelp, which selects popular dishes around you! Select a city to view some stats about what types of dishes are in the area.

Right now, Dishly is in the demo phase of its implementation, so there are only a few cities to choose from. Apologies!
